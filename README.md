# Breast-Cancer-Classification
## There are two types of breast tumor;
### 1. Benign tumor
### 2. Malignant tumor

## Among the differences among the two tumors include;
### Benign tumor 
    - Non Cancerous
    - Capsulated
    - Non-invasive
    - Slow growing
    - Do not metastssize(spred to other body parts)
    - cells are normal
### Malignant tumor
    - Cancerous
    - Non-capsulated 
    - Fast growing 
    - Metastasize (spreads to other body parts)
    - Cells are large, dark nuclei; nay have abnormal shape
